# Fashion Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard 👕📊

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze fashion retail business performance from **2020 to 2024**.

The dashboard combines sales analysis, profitability monitoring, product insights, country performance analysis, and customer purchasing behavior to support business decision-making.

---

## 🚨 Business Problem

Retail businesses operate across multiple products, countries, sales channels, and customer segments.

However, raw sales data alone cannot easily answer critical business questions such as:

- Which countries generate the strongest sales performance?
- Which product categories drive business growth?
- How profitable are operations?
- What is the impact of returned orders?
- Does discounting influence quantity sold?

This project transforms transactional retail data into meaningful business insights through interactive analytics.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze sales performance between **2020–2024**
- Monitor Revenue, Orders, Quantity Sold, COGS, and Profit
- Compare geographic sales performance
- Evaluate product categories and subcategories
- Analyze returned order behavior
- Study discount impact on customer purchasing patterns

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|------|------|
| Orders | Total completed sales orders |
| QTY | Total units sold |
| Revenue | Total generated sales revenue |
| COGS | Cost of Goods Sold |
| Profit | Net business profit |
| Returned Orders | Returned transaction monitoring |

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Sales Dashboard

- Revenue analysis
- Orders tracking
- Quantity sold monitoring
- Profitability analysis
- Sales by country
- Time intelligence analysis (2020–2024)
- Channel and seasonal filtering

### Product Dashboard

- Product category analysis
- Product subcategory analysis
- Discount vs Quantity correlation analysis
- Returned order tracking
- Product profitability monitoring

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Revenue, Orders, and Profit demonstrate business performance evolution across **2020–2024**.
- Country analysis reveals differences in regional sales contribution.
- Product categories contribute differently to total sales volume.
- Returned orders provide operational performance visibility.
- Discount analysis shows a **very weak negative correlation (-0.02)** with quantity sold.
- Product subcategory insights support better merchandising decisions.
